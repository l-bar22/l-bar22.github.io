About Me

Hello World!!

This is the very first line of code I ever saw, and since then I have loved it. I discovered the world of development for the first time in technology class at school, and it fascinated me, we had as a project to create a web page using html (we used Notepad !!) and that's how I created my first webpage.I had always liked the artistic and design world so discovering that technology could express it in another way made me loved it even more.

However, at first I wasn't sure whether or not I should follow this career path and finally I ended up studying Business,administration and management, although it did not captivated me,it made me discover my other passion, statistics.The latter has given me a new vision of society and how data can be used to understand how, why and for what the world works for and to predict future events and react to them.

So ,after some time working in the financial world, I decided to change my professional path and dedicate myself to what I really like, the technological world, combining my knowledge in design, development and analytics.

In this last year I have dedicated myself to continue training in different areas and to carry out internships and projects as a freelancer, that have allowed me to gain experience by improving my current skills and learning new ones, gaining different business visions, working with people from other job areas, assisting to recognize problems based on their data and developing new strategies and giving new ideas to achieve their objectives, for this reason I am always open to working in multidisciplinary and dynamic environments that require creativity and analytical skills and thus continue learning and working on what I like the most.
